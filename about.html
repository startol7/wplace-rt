What is TePlace?

TePlace is an online world map game where players paint tiny tiles on the real map and capture territories for their country. It features Today, Week, Month, and All-time leaderboards persisted on your device.

Inspired by community projects like r/place and WPlace, TePlace focuses on geography, rankings, and colorful creativity.

How to Play

  1. Select your country from the header.
  2. Choose a color (updates instantly) and click the map to paint.
  3. Capture enemy tiles and climb the leaderboards.

Notes

Online users count is a demo number. To count real presence, you’ll need a realtime backend (WebSocket, etc.).
