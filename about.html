🎮 What is TePlace?

TePlace is a free multiplayer browser game where players paint territories on a real world map and compete for global domination. Inspired by Reddit's r/place and similar collaborative pixel art projects, TePlace brings the concept to geographic conquest.

🌍 Real World Map

Paint on actual map tiles using OpenStreetMap data

🏆 Global Rankings

Compete in daily, weekly, and monthly leaderboards

🎨 Custom Colors

Choose any color to represent your nation

⚡ Real-time Updates

See other players' moves instantly

📖 How to Play

Getting Started

  1. Choose Your Country: Select your nation from the dropdown menu
  2. Pick a Color: Choose your territory color (each country can use any color)
  3. Start Painting: Click anywhere on the map to claim territories
  4. Watch Your Ranking: Track your progress on the leaderboards

Game Mechanics

  • Tile Size: Each tile is 0.0005° (approximately 50m x 50m at the equator)
  • Brush Sizes: Paint 1×1, 2×2, or 3×3 tile areas
  • Cooldowns: 10s for 1×1, 40s for 2×2, 90s for 3×3
  • Territory Capture: Paint over enemy tiles to capture them

Strategy Tips

  • 🎯 Focus on clusters: Build connected territories for better defense
  • ⏰ Time your attacks: Coordinate with teammates during active hours
  • 🗺️ Choose strategic locations: Cities and landmarks are high-value targets
  • 🎨 Use distinctive colors: Make your territories easily recognizable

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is TePlace free to play?

Yes! TePlace is completely free with no hidden costs or pay-to-win mechanics.

Do I need to create an account?

You can play as a guest, but signing in (anonymously) saves your progress and territories.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes! TePlace is fully optimized for mobile browsers with touch controls.

How are rankings calculated?

Rankings are based on the total number of territories your country controls.

Can I form alliances?

While there's no formal alliance system, players often coordinate strategies!
